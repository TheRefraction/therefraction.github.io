ABOUT DESTORY


~ What the hell is this game?!


DESTORY is an RPG inspired by Undertale, Earthbound and some other indie games.

The story takes place in the Sotta Kingdom, a world with a lot of funny and... strange? characters.

The rules of this game are simple.

It's only a matter of choices and consequences.

Dance with a ghost, fly with a fairy, or play a game with a witch who is a TV-presenter.

Do whatever you want.

But don't forget, you are NOT above consequences.

Also, foes in this game are not that bad.

They're actually very friendly.

They only want to be SPARED.

But... it's not that easy to show mercy to an enemy.

So, what will you do?

Will you SPARE them, FLEE or FIGHT?

It's up to you!
